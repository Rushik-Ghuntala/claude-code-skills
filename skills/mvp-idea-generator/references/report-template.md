## Quality Standards

Before presenting the report, verify:
- [ ] Every pain point cites a specific source (platform + URL or quote)
- [ ] Pricing is benchmarked against real competitors (not guessed)
- [ ] $10K MRR math is explicitly shown (N customers × $X = $10K)
- [ ] Growth strategy includes specific channels and tactics (not generic "social media marketing")
- [ ] Risk assessment includes realistic risks (not softballs)
- [ ] Similar products are real, named companies (not hypothetical)
- [ ] MVP features are ruthlessly scoped (3-5, not 10+)
- [ ] Out-of-scope features are listed (shows disciplined thinking)
- [ ] Each idea has a clear differentiator from existing solutions
- [ ] Timeline is realistic for a solo developer or small team
