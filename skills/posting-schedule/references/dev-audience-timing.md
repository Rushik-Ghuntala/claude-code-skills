# Developer Audience Timing Guide

## Peak Posting Windows (US Eastern Time)

### Window 1: Pre-Standup (8:00-9:30 AM ET)
- **Why**: Developers check Twitter before morning standup
- **Audience**: US East Coast + Europe (afternoon)
- **Best for**: Threads, tutorials, educational content
- **Engagement pattern**: Steady build through morning, peaks around 9 AM

### Window 2: Lunch Break (12:00-1:00 PM ET)
- **Why**: Break from work, scrolling mode, opinionated energy
- **Audience**: All US timezones overlapping
- **Best for**: Hot takes, debates, questions, polls
- **Engagement pattern**: Sharp spike, quick decay (people go back to work)

### Window 3: End of Work (5:00-6:30 PM ET)
- **Why**: Wrapping up work, reflective mood, sharing wins
- **Audience**: US-focused (Europe is offline)
- **Best for**: Build-in-public updates, announcements, stories
- **Engagement pattern**: Moderate spike, carries into evening

### Window 4: Evening Wind-Down (9:00-10:30 PM ET)
- **Why**: Relaxing, longer attention spans, deeper engagement
- **Audience**: US + late-night global devs
- **Best for**: Stories, personal content, memes, lighter takes
- **Engagement pattern**: Slower but deeper engagement (longer dwell time)

## Day-of-Week Patterns

| Day | Dev Activity Level | Best Content | Notes |
|-----|-------------------|-------------|-------|
| Monday | High (fresh week) | Threads, tutorials | People are motivated to learn |
| Tuesday | Highest | Any format | Peak developer Twitter activity |
| Wednesday | High | Code tips, takes | Hump day — quick value wins |
| Thursday | Moderate-High | BIP, announcements | Pre-Friday energy |
| Friday | Moderate | Community, memes | Winding down, social mood |
| Saturday | Low-Moderate | Stories, side projects | Hobbyist devs active |
| Sunday | Low | Rest or light engagement | Lowest activity — save energy |

## Timezone Adjustments

### Primarily US Audience
- Use the windows above as-is
- Tuesday-Thursday = highest engagement days

### Primarily European Audience
- Shift all windows 5-6 hours earlier (UTC)
- Morning: 7:00-8:30 AM UTC (before work)
- Lunch: 11:00 AM-12:00 PM UTC
- Evening: 5:00-7:00 PM UTC

### Primarily Indian Audience
- Morning: 8:00-9:30 AM IST (UTC+5:30)
- Lunch: 12:30-1:30 PM IST
- Evening: 7:00-9:00 PM IST

### Global Developer Audience
- **Best overlap window**: 8:00-10:00 AM ET (1:00-3:00 PM UTC)
- This catches: US morning, Europe afternoon, India evening
- Tuesdays and Wednesdays have the most global overlap

## Seasonal Patterns
- **Conference weeks**: Post during/immediately after keynotes (live commentary gets massive reach)
- **Holiday weeks** (US): Lower competition, can get outsized reach with less effort
- **Year-end** (Dec): Prediction and review content performs exceptionally well
- **Summer** (Jun-Aug): Slightly lower engagement, but less competition — good time to build habits

## Algorithm Considerations
- Post when YOUR followers are most active (not just general peak times)
- Consistency matters more than perfect timing — same time daily builds audience habits
- First 30 minutes of engagement determine algorithmic distribution
- Seed engagement: have 2-3 friends/mutuals engage early to boost initial signals
