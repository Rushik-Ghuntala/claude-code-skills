# Weekly Content Cadence

## Standard Weekly Schedule

### Monday — Thread Day
- **Pillar**: Educational
- **Why Monday**: People start the week in learning mode. Threads get morning engagement and carry through the day.
- **Content**: 5-12 tweet thread on a technical topic
- **Posting time**: 8:00-9:30 AM ET
- **Effort**: High (this is your flagship weekly content)
- **Goal**: Establish authority, drive bookmarks, attract new followers through shares

### Tuesday — Hot Take Day
- **Pillar**: Thought Leadership
- **Why Tuesday**: Mid-week energy is high, people have opinions and time to debate
- **Content**: Concise contrarian opinion or industry take
- **Posting time**: 12:00-1:00 PM ET (lunch debate time)
- **Effort**: Low-Medium (1 well-crafted tweet)
- **Goal**: Spark discussion, drive replies (54x algorithm weight), increase visibility

### Wednesday — Code Tip Day
- **Pillar**: Educational
- **Why Wednesday**: Hump day — people want quick, actionable value
- **Content**: Code snippet, TIL, or quick technical tip
- **Posting time**: 8:00-9:00 AM ET
- **Effort**: Low (1 tweet with code)
- **Goal**: Drive bookmarks (40x signal), establish as go-to technical resource

### Thursday — Build in Public Day
- **Pillar**: Building in Public
- **Why Thursday**: End-of-week reflection. People appreciate transparency as the week winds down.
- **Content**: Project update, metrics share, behind-the-scenes, or lesson learned
- **Posting time**: 5:00-6:30 PM ET (end of workday, reflective mood)
- **Effort**: Low-Medium (authentic update)
- **Goal**: Build loyal community, attract fellow builders, humanize your brand

### Friday — Community Day
- **Pillar**: Community
- **Why Friday**: Weekend approaching, collaborative energy, people are generous with engagement
- **Content**: Quote tweet with insight, shoutout, community question, or recommendation
- **Posting time**: 9:00-10:00 AM ET
- **Effort**: Low (engagement-focused, not creation-focused)
- **Goal**: Build Real-graph scores, strengthen network, reciprocal growth

### Saturday — Story Day
- **Pillar**: Personal
- **Why Saturday**: Weekend mode — people are relaxed and open to personal content
- **Content**: Personal story, career reflection, or relatable developer moment
- **Posting time**: 10:00-11:00 AM ET
- **Effort**: Low-Medium (be authentic, not polished)
- **Goal**: Emotional connection, follower retention, humanize brand

### Sunday — Rest Day
- **Pillar**: None (light engagement only)
- **Activity**: Reply to comments on the week's content, bookmark ideas for next week
- **Goal**: Recharge, maintain presence without creating new content

## Adaptation by Growth Stage

### 0-1K Followers
- **Modify**: Replace Thursday BIP with an extra Community/Reply day
- **Add**: 20+ high-quality replies per day across ALL days
- **Focus**: 70% effort on replies, 30% on original content
- **Thread**: Keep weekly thread but make it shorter (5-7 tweets)

### 1K-10K Followers
- **Standard cadence** as above
- **Add**: 5-10 strategic replies per day
- **Upgrade**: Thursday can alternate between BIP and a second thread

### 10K-50K Followers
- **Add**: One X Space or collaboration per month
- **Upgrade**: Monday thread can be longer (10-15 tweets)
- **Add**: Occasional article (1-2 per month) on article-generator days

### 50K+ Followers
- **Add**: Newsletter promotion woven into Thursday/Friday
- **Upgrade**: Consider daily threads (your content carries enough weight)
- **Add**: Curated content from your community (amplify others)
