Invoke the `mvp-idea-generator` skill to research pain points, trending problems, and market gaps across Reddit, Hacker News, X/Twitter, ProductHunt, IndieHackers, G2, TrustPilot, GitHub, and LinkedIn. Generates scored MVP ideas with detailed reports including pain points, target users, features, pricing, competition, growth strategy, and a month-by-month path to $10K MRR.

Usage: `/mvp-ideas [count] [niche]`

Examples:
- `/mvp-ideas` — broad scan, 5-10 ideas (default)
- `/mvp-ideas developer tools` — focus on developer tooling gaps, 5-10 ideas
- `/mvp-ideas 3` — broad scan, exactly 3 ideas
- `/mvp-ideas 5 developer tools` — exactly 5 ideas focused on developer tooling
- `/mvp-ideas 10 AI tools` — exactly 10 AI-powered product opportunities
- `/mvp-ideas 3 HR tech` — exactly 3 HR and recruiting pain points
- `/mvp-ideas 7 e-commerce Shopify` — exactly 7 ideas in the Shopify ecosystem
- `/mvp-ideas 5 fintech freelancers` — exactly 5 financial tools for freelancers
